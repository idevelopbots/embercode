Ember Commands

Available system commands

/ad-channel
What it does: Set the channel that you want Ember to send the ads.
Who can use them: Server Owners / Anyone with Administrator
/community-ad
What it does: Send your ad in the community channel.
Who can use them: Anyone
/reminder
What it does: The bot reminds to you do something in x time.
Who can use them: Anyone
