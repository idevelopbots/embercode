General Expectations

Standards of conduct, professionalism, and staff behavior across the Las Vegas Roleplay network.

• Always maintain a professional tone when dealing with players.

• Remain impartial and unbiased during disputes.

Driving Expectations

Vehicle operation guidelines, pursuit limitations, and realistic driving standards for staff and community members.

• Staff vehicles must follow realistic physics guidelines during active roleplay.

• Do not use administrative abilities to give an unfair advantage during pursuits.

Mod Calls

In-depth procedures for handling reports, managing tickets, providing player assistance, and escalation paths.

• Always record video evidence before handling an active moderation incident.

• Claim tickets in the queue strictly in chronological order.

Punishments

Reference matrix for disciplinary procedures, ban durations, warning system, and enforcement policies.

• First offense VDM/RDM: Official Warning.
